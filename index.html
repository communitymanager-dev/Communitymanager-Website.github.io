Moderation

/kick <user> [reason]

Kicks a user. Requires Kick role.

/ban <user> [reason]

Bans permanently. Requires Ban role.

/mute <user> <minutes> [reason]

Mutes temporarily. Role: Mute.

/unmute <user>

Removes a mute. Role: Unmute.

/warn <user> [reason]

Warn user. Role: Warn.

/warnlist <user>

Show all warnings. Role: WarnView.

/clear <channel> <amount>

Delete messages. Role: Clear.

/clear2

Delete all messages in channel. Role: Clear2.

/nuke

Reset a channel. Role: Nuke.

Tickets

/ticketsetup <category> [staff_roles]

Setup ticket system. Admin only.

/ticket

Open ticket.

/close

Close ticket (owner/staff).

/reopen

Reopen ticket (staff only).

/delete

Delete ticket (staff only).
